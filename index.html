About Us

SteelCraft PEB Ltd. Began with a passion for craftsmanship. This passion led its founder and Managing Director Engr. Md. Golam Hossain to start a business that provided services and products to diverse industries in need of custom steel fabrication. SteelCraft PEB Ltd. is a sister concern of "Tanjim Group" and incorporated as a private limited company on 27th of January 2016. Since inception Engr. Md. Golam Hossain has strived to maintain a company culture that emphasizes a high level of craftsmanship and enjoyment in work with latest modern Machineries& Technology along with most experienced and self-recognized professionals. His team consists of a fight-knit group of individuals who are meticulous and enthusiastic about doing great work.
